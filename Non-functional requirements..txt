a. Performance Requirements

  1. The system shall respond to user requests within 3 seconds under normal load.

  11. The system shall perform automatic daily data backups.

  13. The system shall prevent data loss during unexpected shutdowns.

  14. The system interface shall be easy to use for non-technical staff.

e. Scalability Requirements

  18. The system shall support future integration with additional hospital departments.
